Guilds

Game Story: In both Guild Wars and Guild Wars 2 video games, the game story has several groups of people that work together to achieve their goals called "guilds". Fellow Players: Create your own guild to do many different in-game activities together.

There are many in-game benefits for joining a Guild. Personally it is an amazing place to make friends, and have memorable experiences.

A common way for players to communicate is through chatting via messages in the game, or by meeting outside the game on Discord to be able to chat while playing the game.
